Control Wear in action

The context

Dino is furious against our administration and wants to enter our office that is now closed to public.

Presence

Dino looks at me and I can see that he is very upset. He is threatening me and repeats that he is determined to go inside despite the closing hours.

Communication

Dino does not listen. He rejects my solutions and all the evidences that the office is closed. He tries to intimidate me verbally.

Assaultive behavior

Dino becomes assaultive and grabs the collar of my jacket. At this very moment, he oversteps the red line.
I need to control him for my own security and because I am on duty (I can't let him go in).

Soft control

I don't want to harm him nor to get hurt. I use Control Wear techniques to leverage on his clothes.

Hard control

Dino is now firmly controlled on the ground with the right amount of force and agrees on not offering resistance anymore.
I try to reestablish communication and to make him calm down. He accepts to comply eventually.

Pressure release

I keep a tight control (adaptated to the indiviual and the context) while inviting him to return when the office is open to public. The job is done and Dino has not been brutalized.

I have handled the situation professionally.
